About Us

Pick-A-Pet was established in 2020 by Jason Hunter and his fiance Jamie Sobieck with the family in mind. They wanted to find a sustainable way to cater to family's needs for a pet and source them responsibly. They searched the country for small breeding operations and established relationships with farms and families who have love for the animals they breed.

In the summer of 2022, during their travels abroad with their 2 children, they found that outside of cats and dogs, other exotic pets could be sourced similarly. They established relationships with breeders of different species from around the globe; along with learning the laws, regulations, and customs for owning particular species within the US. It was during that time they expanded Pick-A-Pet beyond cats, dogs, and hamsters. The roster now includes everything from Sugar Gliders to Capuchin Monkeys.

If you find yourself or someone you know ready to take on a new pet or add to your family, we'd like for you to consider Pick-A-Pet. Happy Picking!!

- Jason & Jamie
